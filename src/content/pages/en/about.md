Hi! I'm **Eugene Petriyov** (Евгений Петриёв), an engineering manager and head of development. I lead software engineering teams and build IoT platforms — from firmware and telemetry pipelines to cloud services and dashboards.

I've spent over a decade in software engineering, growing from a backend engineer through team lead to head of development. I care equally about both sides of the job: the architecture of distributed systems — and the people who build them.

## What I do

- Build and scale engineering teams: hiring, onboarding, career tracks.
- Own architecture and reliability of IoT platforms: devices, telemetry, cloud.
- Shape processes that help rather than hinder: code review, ADRs, incident management.
